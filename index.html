What is PageDAO?

PageDAO is a web3 writer's organization dedicated to building free open source publishing solutions featuring decentralized technology.

What products exist in the decentralized publishing space today?

There are a number of solutions with varying features, but there are still gaps in the market. Fully open, free to mint PDF-based flipbook NFTs can be created from PageDAO's first product, the 2021 ReadMe Books NFTBook minter: https://mint.nftbookbazaar.com. Alexandria Labs, https://alexandriabooks.com has a compelling solution featuring encryption and a bespoke publishing house.

What are the benefits of decentralization?

The primary benefit of decentralization in publishing is that the content is immutable. This means you won't lose access to your work, people can't change it after it gets published, and users actually own and can trade even your digital works. Additionally, costs are lower to publish new books.

Does it cost less to publish onchain?

The marginal cost of publishing a new book trends toward zero as the technology to do so becomes more available.

How does PageDAO work?

PageDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) revolutionizing the publishing industry using blockchain technology. It provides tools for writers, creators, and readers to mint literary NFTs, protect intellectual property, and earn fair royalties. Operating with the $PAGE token, PageDAO is a member-driven cooperative that enables community collaboration, transparent governance, and equitable compensation.

What is the $PAGE token?

The $PAGE token is PageDAO’s native cryptocurrency. It is used for governance, staking rewards, and as a means of exchange for services and assets on the PageDAO platform. Members earn $PAGE tokens by participating in DAO initiatives.

How does PageDAO support creators?

PageDAO empowers creators by offering: Blockchain-based tools to mint literary NFTs and retain ownership. Transparent royalty tracking and smart contract-based payments. Immutable records of ownership, attribution, and usage rights. A censorship-resistant platform for free expression and creativity. Whether you're a writer, illustrator, editor, or reader, PageDAO ensures fair recognition and compensation for all.

How is PageDAO different?

PageDAO prioritizes fairness, transparency, and innovation. By leveraging blockchain technology, it eliminates middlemen, ensures equitable royalty distribution, and fosters a collaborative environment for creators. With partnerships like Vagobond Magazine and Whitney Morgan Media, PageDAO is building a sustainable, decentralized literary ecosystem for writers and readers alike.
